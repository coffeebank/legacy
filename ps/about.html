About Us

Private Scouts is a website first envisioned in 2008 for entertainment purposes. Our goal was to create and maintain a website where you can have fun and use tools at the same time. As of July 2014, Private Scouts has been remodeled into a new website called PrivScouts, while the original became Legacy Private Scouts. As of October 2015, Private Scouts has merged with Explarcade.

 

Accessing Old Pages

Edit: As of 27 March, 2020, all services have been retired.


All old pages can still be accessed as long as you have a direct URL to that webpage. Some old pages can also be accessible from the apps available in the Legacy App Center.


Some pages still require membership to access.
